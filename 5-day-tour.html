The Adventure: 5-Day Desert Passage

Crafted for the intrepid traveler, our 5-day desert passage from Marrakech to Fes is a journey of dramatic contrasts. This itinerary is focused on the sublime landscapes of southern Morocco, offering a deep immersion into the world of kasbahs, gorges, and the Sahara.

Key Experiences:

  • Traverse the High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n'Tichka pass.
  • Explore the UNESCO-protected kasbah of Ait Benhaddou.
  • Spend a memorable night in a luxury Berber camp in the Merzouga dunes.
  • Enjoy a full day of 4x4 exploration around the Erg Chebbi dunes.
  • Journey through the beautiful Ziz Valley with its lush palm groves.

Full Daily Itinerary

Day 1: Marrakech to Dades Valley

We depart from Marrakech, ascending into the High Atlas Mountains. After a tour of Ait Benhaddou, our route continues through Ouarzazate to the spectacular Dades Valley, where we will spend the night.

Day 2: Dades Valley to Merzouga Dunes

After exploring the Todra Gorge, our journey leads to the Sahara. Upon reaching the dunes of Erg Chebbi, you will embark on a camel trek to your luxury desert camp for an unforgettable evening.

Day 3: Full-Day Sahara Desert Exploration

This day is dedicated to discovering the desert. We will traverse the dunes by 4x4, visit the village of Khamlia to experience Gnawa music, and share traditional mint tea with a local nomad family.

Day 4: Merzouga to Midelt

After witnessing a magnificent desert sunrise, we travel north through the expansive Ziz Valley. Our day concludes in the mountain town of Midelt, known for its apple orchards.

Day 5: Midelt to Fes

Our final day takes us through the cedar forests of the Middle Atlas, with a chance to see Barbary macaques. We then descend towards the ancient city of Fes, where your tour concludes.
