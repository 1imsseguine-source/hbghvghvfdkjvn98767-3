The Definitive: 13-Day Grand Morocco Discovery

For the discerning traveler seeking a comprehensive and unhurried exploration, our 13-day expedition from Casablanca represents the pinnacle of Moroccan travel. This is a masterful itinerary that weaves together every essential thread of the Moroccan tapestry.

Key Experiences:

  • An exploration of all four Imperial Cities: Rabat, Meknes, Fes, and Marrakech.
  • A visit to the iconic blue city of Chefchaouen.
  • A historical tour of the Roman ruins of Volubilis.
  • A multi-day immersion in the Sahara with a luxury camp stay.
  • Relaxation in the charming coastal and artistic town of Essaouira.
  • Includes some of our most popular Morocco day tours from Marrakech.

Grand Tour Itinerary Overview

This is a high-level summary of your expansive journey. Each day is meticulously planned to ensure a comfortable pace and maximum discovery.

  • Days 1-3: Your journey begins in Casablanca. We proceed to the capital, Rabat, and then north to the stunning blue city of Chefchaouen in the Rif Mountains.
  • Days 4-6: We explore the Roman ruins at Volubilis and the imperial city of Meknes before arriving in Fes for a full guided tour. The journey then turns south towards the Sahara Desert.
  • Days 7-9: After your Sahara experience, we travel via the Road of a Thousand Kasbahs to Marrakech, where you will enjoy a comprehensive guided city tour.
  • Days 10-13: The final days include a custom day trip from Marrakech, a visit to the breezy coastal town of Essaouira, and a final return to Casablanca for your departure.
