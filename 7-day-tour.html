The Classic: 7-Day Imperial Cities & Sahara Tour

This is our signature itinerary and the definitive introduction to Morocco. Our 7-day tour is meticulously designed for the traveler who wishes to experience the kingdom's most renowned cultural and natural highlights in a seamless, one-week journey.

Key Experiences:

  • A privately guided exploration of the ancient Fes medina.
  • A sunset camel expedition into the Erg Chebbi dunes.
  • An overnight stay in a luxury Berber desert encampment.
  • A scenic drive through the Todra Gorges and Dades Valley.
  • A visit to the UNESCO World Heritage site of Ait Benhaddou.
  • An immersion into the vibrant atmosphere of Marrakech.

Full Daily Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Welcome to Fes

Upon your arrival at Fes-Sa√Øss Airport, your private guide will welcome you and provide a comfortable transfer to your traditional Riad within the historic medina.

Day 2: Cultural Immersion in Fes

A full-day guided tour dedicated to Fes, the spiritual heart of Morocco. You will explore the famous tanneries, intricate souks, madrasas, and the majestic Royal Palace gates.

Day 3: Journey Across the Atlas to the Sahara

We travel south, crossing the Middle Atlas mountains and passing through cedar forests. As we approach the desert, you will begin your sunset camel trek to a luxury camp for a magical night under the stars.

Day 4: Gorges and the Road of a Thousand Kasbahs

After a desert sunrise, our journey continues to the dramatic Todra Gorges. We then travel along the famous "Road of a Thousand Kasbahs," a scenic route dotted with ancient fortified villages.

Day 5: Ait Benhaddou and Ouarzazate

We explore Ouarzazate, the "Hollywood of Africa," and visit the magnificent Ait Benhaddou, a striking example of southern Moroccan architecture and a famous film location.

Day 6: Over the High Atlas to Marrakech

A spectacular drive takes us over the High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n'Tichka pass, descending into the plains and arriving in the vibrant "Red City" of Marrakech.

Day 7: Departure from Marrakech

Enjoy a final Moroccan breakfast before we transfer you to Marrakech-Menara Airport for your departure, concluding your classic Moroccan journey.
